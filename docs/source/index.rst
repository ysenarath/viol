Welcome to Viol documentation!
===================================

Welcome to Viol's documentation. 
Viol is a extention to [Flask](https://flask.palletsprojects.com/en/stable/) to 
easily integrate [HTMX](https://htmx.org/). It is designed to make getting started 
quick and easy, with the ability to scale up to complex applications.
